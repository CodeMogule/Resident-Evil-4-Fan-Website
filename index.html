About

Following the unprecedented disaster of the Raccoon City Incident, the Umbrella Corporation has collapsed under the weight of public outrage and government intervention. Leon S. Kennedy survived the city's fall, and has now been assigned as a special agent reporting to the President of the United States. After the President's daughter is kidnapped, Leon is tasked with following eyewitness reports leading him to a small village in Europe to look for her. Even the horrors of Raccoon City couldn't prepare Leon for the secrets he would soon discover...

characters

Leon S. Kennedy

Leon is the main protagonist. In 2004, he is a special agent assigned to rescue the U.S. president's daughter Ashley Graham who is being held somewhere in Europe.

Ashley Graham

Ashley Graham is the daughter of former US President Graham. She was briefly held captive by the Spanish cult Los Iluminados as a means of gaining control over the Presidency.

Bitores Mendez

A tall, imposing man with an unkempt beard and wearing a dirty trenchcoat, this man is a leading member of the Los Illuminados and the chief of a small rural village.

Ada Wong

Ada Wong is the pseudonym of an enigmatic unnamed spy of Asian-American descent. She has gained notoriety in the corporate world for being able to handle serious situation.

Osmund Saddler

Osmund Saddler, often referred to as Lord Saddler, was the leader of Los Iluminados, a neopagan cult and paramilitary organisation.

Luis Sera

Dr. Luis Sera was a biologist native to an isolated region of Spain. He was recruited into Los Iluminados' research department to genetically-engineer Plagas

Jack krauser

Krauser took part in the infiltration of Los Iluminados to obtain a Plaga parasite. Krauser was killed in late 2004 by Ada Wong, who was on orders by her agency to steal the parasite.
